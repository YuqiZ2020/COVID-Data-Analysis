COVID-Mortality Risk Score

Github @YuqiZ2020 Github Repository

I collected prevalence statistics for 51 states including: - census information: Age, Gender, Ethnicity - Health conditions: BMI, Blood Pressure, Cancer, Diabetes, etc. Data Source: - Demographics: American Community Survey (2018) - Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) Prevalence Data (2018) - Social Deprivation Index (SDI) from Robert Graham Center - United States Cancer Statistics - The National Health Interview Survey (For Age Stratified Cancer Data) I calculated the index of excess risk of 51 states with these data. States with high relative risks: New Mexico, Florida, Mississippi
