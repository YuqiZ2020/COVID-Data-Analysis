COVID Mortality Risk Score

This project calculates COVID mortality risk score of people in different locations, and provide a calculator for individuals to evaluate their risk. I worked on data collection and community-level data analysis. Our publication: Jin, J., Agarwala, N., Kundu, P., Harvey, B., Zhang, Y., Wallace, E., & Chatterjee, N. Individual and community-level risk for COVID-19 mortality in the United States. Nat Med (2020). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41591-020-01191-8
